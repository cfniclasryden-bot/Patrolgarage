01 — Our Story

Why We
Exist.

Most workshops treat the Patrol like any other SUV. But Patrols aren't generic vehicles. The Y61 and Y62 have their own quirks, failure points, and fixes that only people who've worked on hundreds of them know.

We built our workshop in Ras Al Khor specifically around servicing this car—the right tools, the right parts supply, and mechanics who can diagnose problems from the sound the engine makes. We've earned our reputation as the go-to Patrol specialists not through marketing, but through ten years of honest work.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of Patrol owners across Dubai and the UAE. From routine maintenance to complex engine work, suspension upgrades to full restorations, we've become the workshop Patrol owners trust.

03 — Why Patrols

Specialization
Matters.

The Nissan Patrol is an icon. Built tough, trusted by millions, perfectly suited to the UAE's climate and terrain. But that toughness comes with complexity.

Patrols aren't cookie-cutter vehicles. Y61s are different from Y62s. Diesel engines differ from petrol. Off-road Patrols differ from daily drivers. To properly service and repair one, you need more than generic mechanic skills. You need passion, specialization, and deep hands-on experience.

When you bring your Patrol to us, you're not getting a "generic vehicle technician." You're getting a Patrol specialist who understands this engine, this platform, and the unique challenges UAE owners face.
