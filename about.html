Why We Specialize in Patrols

The Nissan Patrol is an icon. It's built tough, trusted by millions worldwide, and uniquely suited to the UAE's climate and terrain. But that toughness comes with complexity—and Patrols aren't cookie-cutter vehicles. Y61s are different from Y62s. Diesel engines differ from petrol. Off-road Patrols differ from daily drivers.

To properly service and repair a Patrol, you need more than generic mechanic skills. You need passion, specialization, and deep hands-on experience. That's what we bring to the workshop every day. When you bring your Patrol to us, you're not getting a "generic vehicle technician"—you're getting a Patrol specialist.
