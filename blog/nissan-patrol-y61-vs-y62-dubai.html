Y61 vs Y62·Comparison

Which Patrol?

Y61 vs Y62: The Core Difference

Y61 (1997–2004) is older, simpler, more basic. Y62 (2010-present) is newer, more complex, more features. Y61 is more durable in dusty conditions. Y62 is more reliable mechanically but has more electronic failures. For Dubai buyers, the choice depends on budget and use case. Y61 suits off-roaders who maintain themselves. Y62 suits daily drivers wanting comfort and reliability.

Engine & Power

Y61 offers 4.8L petrol (210 hp) or 3.0L turbo diesel (160 hp). Y62 offers 5.6L petrol (400 hp) or 4.0L turbo diesel (250 hp). Y62 is significantly more powerful and efficient. Y62 fuel consumption is better despite more power. Y62 has turbo diesel option which Y61 lacks. For UAE towing and hauling, Y62 diesel is superior. Y61 is adequate for regular driving but feels slower.

Reliability & Common Issues

Y61: Simple mechanics, less electronics, fewer failures. Known issue: gearbox shuddering. Y62: More computer modules, ABS, traction control, more electronics failures possible. Known issues: AC problems, electrical gremlins. Y61 parts are cheaper, easier to replace. Y62 requires computer diagnostics for complex issues. Y61 is more user-maintainable. Y62 needs specialist technician for serious work.

Running Costs in Dubai

Y61 fuel: 10-12 km/liter petrol, 8-10 km/liter diesel. Y62 fuel: 12-14 km/liter petrol, 10-12 km/liter diesel. Service: Y61 AED 800–1,200. Y62 AED 1,200–1,500 (more complex systems). Parts: Y61 cheaper, widely available. Y62 more expensive, genuine parts needed. Repairs: Y61 simpler, often DIY possible. Y62 needs specialists.

Off-Road Capability

Both are excellent off-road. Y61 has simpler suspension, lower tech, more robust. Y62 has electronic traction control, better suspension, more gadgets. Y61 is easier to modify without breaking electronics. Y62 modifications trigger warning lights if done wrong. For serious dune driving, Y61 is more forgiving. Y62 is more capable but requires electronic expertise to modify properly.

Which to Buy?

Buy Y61 if: budget conscious, want simple mechanics, plan DIY maintenance, focus on off-road, love vintage charm. Buy Y62 if: want modern comfort, daily driving, less frequent maintenance, prefer automation, towing capacity matters. For most Dubai buyers: Y62 is the better choice. For enthusiasts: Y61 offers more character and control.
