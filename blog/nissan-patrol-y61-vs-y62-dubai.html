Frequently Asked Questions

Is a Y61 more reliable than a Y62?

Y61s have simpler systems and are generally more reliable if well-maintained. Y62s are more modern but have more electronics that can fail in extreme heat.

Which is better for off-roading?

Both are excellent off-road vehicles. Y62 is more comfortable on long trips, Y61 is simpler to modify and repair in the field.

What's a fair price for a used Y61 vs Y62 in Dubai?

Prices vary by year, mileage, and condition. Generally, older Y61s (2000-2004) fetch AED 40,000-70,000 while newer Y62s (2015+) command AED 80,000-150,000+.
