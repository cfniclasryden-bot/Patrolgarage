Frequently Asked Questions

Should I use 10W40 or 15W50 for my Patrol in Dubai?

For Dubai's extreme heat, 15W50 synthetic is ideal for better protection at high temperatures. 10W40 works in mild seasons but may thin out too much in summer.

Are synthetic oils worth the extra cost?

Yes, in UAE heat. Synthetic oils last 1.5-2x longer, provide better engine protection, and perform better at high temperatures. The extra cost is justified.

How often should I change oil in Dubai?

Every 5,000-7,000km or every 3-4 months for regular driving in hot climate. More frequent changes prevent breakdown in extreme heat.
