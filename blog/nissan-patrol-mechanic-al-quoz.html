Frequently Asked Questions

Is a specialist garage more expensive than general shops?

Specialists charge fair prices because they have Patrol expertise and need fewer diagnostics. General shops often give low quotes but find hidden problems during work.

Should I always use a dealer for warranty work?

No. Independent specialists like Patrol Garage often match or beat dealer prices while giving more personalized service. Many warranty repairs go to aftermarket shops.

How do I know if a quote is fair?

Get multiple quotes for the same work, read the detailed breakdown, and ask questions about each line item. Fair quotes should explain what's included.
