Frequently Asked Questions

What tire pressure should I use for dune driving?

Typically 16-20 PSI for soft sand, depending on weight and tire. Start at manufacturer spec and experiment. Check often for temperature rise.

Do I need a snorkel for desert driving?

Snorkels prevent sand ingestion into the engine on deep water crossings and dusty tracks. Recommended for serious off-road use, not just dunes.

How much does off-road setup cost?

Budget AED 5,000 for basic setup (lift, tires, armor), AED 10,000+ for comprehensive modifications (winch, snorkel, skid plates, suspension).
