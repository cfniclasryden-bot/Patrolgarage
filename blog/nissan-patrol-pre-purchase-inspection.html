Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a pre-purchase inspection cost?

Professional inspection typically costs AED 400-600. Well worth it to avoid a bad purchase that could cost thousands in repairs.

Should I test drive the Patrol on a highway?

Yes. Test highway driving to check acceleration, braking, wind/road noise, vibrations, and how the gearbox performs at speed.

What's the most important thing to check on a used Patrol?

The gearbox. Transmission repairs are expensive (AED 4,000-8,000+). A test drive should reveal any slipping or shifting issues immediately.

What mileage is too much for a Patrol?

Well-maintained Patrols easily exceed 300,000km. Focus on service history rather than mileage. A 250,000km unit serviced regularly is better than a 150,000km neglected one.
