Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a lift kit cost for a Patrol?

Budget AED 2,500 to 5,000+ depending on the kit quality and installation labor. Complete systems with shocks and springs are more expensive than bolt-on spacers.

Will a lift kit void my warranty?

Aftermarket suspension modifications may affect certain warranty coverage. Discuss with your dealer beforehand, or use a trusted installer who understands warranty implications.

How often should suspension be serviced?

Every 20,000km or annually, inspect shocks, springs, and bushings. More frequent checks if you do off-road driving or notice creaking.
