Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most common Y62 problems?

The most common Y62 issues include gearbox shuddering, AC compressor failure, suspension wear, and electrical issues. These often appear in high-mileage Patrols or those used frequently in Dubai heat.

How much does Y62 gearbox repair cost in Dubai?

Gearbox shudder repair typically costs between AED 1,200 and AED 3,500 depending on whether it's a fluid change, solenoid replacement, or more extensive work.

Why do Y62 ACs fail so often in Dubai?

UAE's extreme heat puts constant stress on AC systems. Y62 compressors can fail from overwork, and refrigerant leaks are common in older units. Regular servicing prevents most failures.

Is a used Y62 a good buy?

Y62s are generally reliable vehicles, but mileage and maintenance history matter greatly. Always get a pre-purchase inspection. Well-maintained Y62s easily exceed 300,000km without major issues.
