Service Cost Breakdown

Understanding Patrol Service Costs

Owning a Nissan Patrol comes with responsibilities. Regular maintenance keeps your vehicle running strong and prevents expensive breakdowns. Here's a detailed breakdown of what you'll spend on keeping your Patrol in top condition in Dubai.

Routine Maintenance: Every 10,000 km

The most common service interval for a Patrol is every 10,000 km or 6 months. A standard oil and filter change costs AED 400–700 depending on oil type. We recommend synthetic oil for UAE heat (AED 500–900). Tire rotation and balancing adds AED 200–350. Air filter replacement is AED 150–250. A full routine service typically runs AED 1,000–1,500.

Major Maintenance: Every 40,000 km

At 40,000 km, you're due for a cabin air filter replacement (AED 100–180) and brake fluid check (AED 50 diagnostic). Brake pad inspection and replacement average AED 1,200–1,800 per axle. Transmission fluid check or partial flush costs AED 300–500. Budget AED 2,500–3,500 for a 40k service.

Major Service: Every 80,000 km

The 80,000 km mark is significant. Full transmission fluid and filter change costs AED 1,500–2,200. Spark plugs replacement runs AED 800–1,200. Coolant flush and fill is AED 600–900. Brake system inspection with fluid replacement adds AED 1,200–1,600. Total: AED 4,500–6,000.

AC Service & Refrigerant

UAE heat demands AC attention. Annual AC gas refill and filter replacement costs AED 300–500. Compressor servicing with oil and leak check runs AED 400–700. If compressor replacement is needed, budget AED 1,200–2,500. Many owners do twice-yearly AC service in summer.

Suspension & Steering

Shock absorbers and suspension bushings wear faster in Dubai's heat. Annual inspection is AED 200–300. Shock replacement (per unit) costs AED 600–1,200. Full suspension overhaul with bushings and steering components can run AED 4,000–7,000. Budget for at least one suspension refresh every 5 years.

Brake Service

Brake pads typically last 40,000–60,000 km. Pad replacement averages AED 800–1,400 per axle. Brake fluid bleeding and replacement costs AED 500–800. Full brake overhaul with rotors and calipers runs AED 3,000–5,000. Always address brake issues immediately.

Electrical & Battery

Patrol batteries last 3–4 years in UAE heat. Replacement costs AED 300–600. Alternator service or replacement runs AED 1,200–2,500. Starter motor issues average AED 1,000–1,800. Wiring harness repairs vary widely, AED 500–2,500 depending on location.

Annual Budget Estimate

Light driving (20,000 km/year): AED 2,500–4,000. Moderate driving (30,000 km/year): AED 4,000–6,500. Heavy driving (40,000+ km/year): AED 6,500–10,000. These estimates include routine maintenance but exclude major repairs or accidents. Always budget 10–15% extra for unexpected issues.

Pro tip: Preventive maintenance costs less than emergency repairs. A AED 500 fluid change beats a AED 8,000 transmission rebuild. Track your service history and stick to manufacturer intervals.
