Frequently Asked Questions

What's the cheapest way to service a Patrol in Dubai?

Stick to the service schedule, use genuine parts when critical, and choose a specialist who knows Patrols (avoiding markup shops). Regular maintenance prevents expensive repairs.

Is Y61 or Y62 more expensive to service?

Y62s are slightly more expensive due to more complex systems, but differences are minimal. Both cost roughly AED 800-1,500 for minor service.

Should I use genuine Nissan parts?

For critical components (engine, gearbox, suspension) genuine parts are recommended. For filters and fluids, quality aftermarket is acceptable and saves 20-30%.
