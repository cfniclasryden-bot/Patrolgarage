Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my Patrol AC?

In Dubai, AC service every 6-12 months is recommended. Regular gas top-ups, condenser cleaning, and filter replacement keep your AC efficient in extreme heat.

Why is my Patrol AC blowing warm air?

Three main causes: low refrigerant (leak or normal loss), compressor failure, or blower motor issues. A technician can diagnose with a pressure test.

Can I refill AC gas myself?

AC gas handling requires special equipment and certification in the UAE. It's illegal and dangerous to DIY. Always use a certified technician.
