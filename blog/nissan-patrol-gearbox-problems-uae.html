Frequently Asked Questions

What does gearbox shudder mean?

Shuddering is typically caused by low transmission fluid, worn friction plates, or dirty fluid. It requires immediate attention to prevent failure.

How much does gearbox repair cost in Dubai?

Simple repairs (fluid change, solenoid) run AED 1,200-2,500. Full rebuilds cost AED 4,000-7,000. Replacement gearboxes are even more expensive.

Can I drive with a slipping gearbox?

No. Driving with slipping can cause catastrophic damage. Get it diagnosed and repaired immediately to avoid complete failure.
